The computer has randomly generated a secret combination of 4 colours. You must guess the combination in 8 or fewer tries to win. Make your guesses from the choice of colours on the left-hand side, then submit. Each time you submit a guess, the indicators on the right-hand side will let you know how close you were. Green indicates that your peg is the right color in the right position. Yellow indicates that your peg is the right color in the wrong position. If your guess indicates 4 out 4 green, within 8 tries, you win.
